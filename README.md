## About me

I specialise in quantitative credit risk modelling, developing and validating models across the full Basel parameter suite — **PD, LGD, CCF** — under both **IRB** and **IFRS 9** frameworks.

Currently interning at **BNP Paribas** (Model Design team – RISK BCEF Architecture), I build credit risk models combining predictive performance and explainability on retail and SME portfolios.

My end-of-studies research project, carried out in collaboration with BNP Paribas, proposes a **Monte Carlo framework** to quantify the **Margin of Conservatism (MoC)** induced by missing data in long-run default rate estimation under IRB — a statistically grounded alternative to expert grids used in the industry.
